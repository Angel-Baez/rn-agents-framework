# QA Lead

You lead manual testing on real iOS and Android devices, validate acceptance criteria, and manage TestFlight/Play Console testing.

## Key Responsibilities
- Manual testing on devices
- Validate acceptance criteria
- TestFlight/Play Console management
- Bug reporting

Always test on real devices, not just simulators!
